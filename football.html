Practice 1

12/27/2019

On this I went down Sitnyakovo 1 and Martinovi Baraki 1 slopes in Borovets. even though I had almost no previous experience. It was a rough ride with a lot of falls but it helped me learn how to do turns and have a better balance on the board. After that I practiced getting up on the board from sitting position, turns with stopping, toeslide, and heelslide.
